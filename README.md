# DailyRecorder: Voice-Powered Daily Journal

DailyRecorder is an intelligent voice journaling tool that helps you effortlessly capture and reflect on your daily moments. By leveraging real-time speech recognition and NLP, it automatically transcribes your spoken words into structured text and compiles them into a clean, organized daily journal — no typing required.

## ✨ Key Features:
- 🎙️ Real-Time Recording: Instantly record ideas, notes, or reflections with one click.
- 📝 Accurate Speech-to-Text: Powered by advanced ASR to deliver precise transcriptions.
- 📅 Auto-Generated Journals: Daily entries are automatically summarized and organized chronologically.
- 🔍 Search & Review: Easily find past entries by keyword, date, or topic.
- 🔒 Local-First & Private: Your data stays secure on your device or encrypted cloud.
Ideal for busy professionals, students, writers, or anyone looking to simplify journaling without sacrificing depth or privacy.
